Why Stack Sats?

Most people will never own a full bitcoin. That's fine. Bitcoin is divisible to 8 decimal places, and the smallest unit, a satoshi, is how most people accumulate. Small, consistent purchases over time is one of the most common ways to build a Bitcoin position without trying to time the market.
